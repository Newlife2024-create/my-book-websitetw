The Misconception of an Unpaved Path

About the Book

The Misconception of an Unpaved Path: Breaking New Ground by Dr. Timothy Whitley is a transformative guide that merges biblical wisdom with personal anecdotes to inspire readers on their journey of self-discovery and spiritual growth. This book explores common struggles like self-doubt, patience, and resilience while emphasizing faith-driven personal growth.

About the Author

Dr. Timothy Whitley shares his personal experiences and insights in this faith-driven journey. His work encourages readers to embrace life's challenges and trust in God's timing, offering a framework for a purpose-filled life.
